About Us 🐾

🐾 What is MagicMeow?

Meow meow! 🐱 MagicMeow is a time management tool that combines an AI assistant with a gamified experience, designed to help you plan tasks efficiently while keeping you motivated and having fun. Whether it’s daily studying, work scheduling, or long-term goal tracking, MagicMeow makes time management smarter, more intuitive, and super easy!

🌟 Our Vision

In this fast-paced world, we believe time management shouldn’t just be a cold to-do list—it should be an interactive, motivating, and comforting process. MagicMeow aims to help users build better habits, achieve their goals, and feel a sense of accomplishment and joy along the way, with the help of AI and adorable virtual assistants. Purr-fect, right? 🐾

🚀 Our Features

  • AI Task Assistant: Meow the Cat & Bunny the Rabbit are your smart buddies, helping you plan tasks.
  • Quadrant Time Management: Prioritize tasks based on importance and urgency to boost efficiency.
  • Focus Mode: Features like Pomodoro timers, white noise, and focus lock to help you work deeply.
  • Gamified Motivation: Complete tasks to unlock badges, rewards, and interact with your AI buddies!
  • Smart Schedule Adjustments: AI learns your habits and optimizes your daily plans.
